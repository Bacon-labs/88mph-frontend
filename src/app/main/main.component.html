Getting started

How does 88mph work?

ðŸ’€ This project is in beta. Use at your own risk.

1. Deposit

Connect your Ethereum wallet to 88mph and deposit your DAI into the pool. Your deposit is locked in 88mph's pool for a minimum of 91 days. A longer lock period means a larger amount of upfront fixed-rate interest.

2. Earn {{oneYearInterestRate.toFormat(DECIMALS)}}% upfront fixed-rate interest

Here's the cool part: You get the interest upfront! It's like your interest is travelling back from the future! While you get your fixed-rate APY instantly, your deposit will generate interests during the lock period for the 88mph's pool at a variable APY on Compound protocol, insuring the stability of 88mph's pool.

3. Withdraw

When the lock period is over, withdraw your initial deposit. If you deposited 100.0000 DAI, yeah, you get back 100.0000 DAI.

88mph fee disclosure

88mph charges a 10% fee on your upfront interest. The fee is already factored into the displayed APY ({{oneYearInterestRate.toFormat(DECIMALS)}}%), so don't worry about doing more math ðŸ˜‰

88mph risk disclosure

If the interest rate offered by 88mph's underlying money market (e.g. Compound) drops too low, and many users try to withdraw at the same time, then 88mph could be temporarily insolvent, forcing users to wait a bit more to be able to withdraw their full deposit.

Even though this risk is present, 88mph is planning to mitigate it with sponsored deposits that help maintain the pool's solvency while offering visibility to 88mph's sponsors.

Sponsored deposits

Sponsored deposits let companies or organizations support 88mph's security in exchange for their visibility on 88mph. Sponsored deposits are like normal deposits, except that you don't get the upfront interest and get counted separately in the statistics.

Sponsoring 88mph won't have a direct cost, since sponsors can withdraw a deposit after its deposit period is over. However, sponsors will be shouldering risk for normal users, and may lose money if a black swan event occurs.
